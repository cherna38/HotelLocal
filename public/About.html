About HotelLocal

Hotel Local is a locally made web app designed to help travels find locally owned and authentic hotels while also supporting family owned business from the competition of high end franchise.

All of our hotels are locally owned and one of a kind, providing guest with the most authentic experience of exploration in their travels. Our hotels have a range of ratings from 1 to five stars and provide all amenities without the tags of a franchise. Currently, we have cities limited to California but our database is always expanding and we hope to cover all of America soon!
